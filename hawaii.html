When Is the Best Time to Visit Hawaii?

Hawaii is a dream destination, but choosing the right time to visit can affect your experience and budget. Whether you want perfect weather, fewer crowds, or the best deals on flights and hotels, this guide will help you plan your trip.

Quick Answer:

The perfect time to go to Hawaii for great weather and fewer tourists is:

  • April to June (Spring)
  • September to mid-December (Fall)

If you’re looking for whale watching or surfing, winter is best, while summer is perfect for families but more crowded.

When to travel to Hawaii for great weather

Hawaii has two main seasons:

Dry Season (April to October)

  • Warm, sunny days (perfect for beaches & outdoor activities)
  • Low chance of rain
  • Best for snorkeling, diving, and hiking

Rainy Season (November to March)

  • Cooler temperatures
  • More rain, but waterfalls are at their best
  • Best for whale watching & big-wave surfing

When to Visit Hawaii to Avoid Crowds

Want to enjoy Hawaii without the long lines and packed beaches?

Best months for fewer crowds:

  • Mid-April to early June
  • September to mid-December

Busiest months:

  • December 20 to early January (Christmas & New Year’s)
  • June to August (Summer break)

Pro Tip: Book flights & hotels at least 3 months in advance during peak seasons!

Cheapest Time to Visit Hawaii

Hawaii can be expensive, but you can save money by timing your trip right.

Cheapest months to visit:

  • April to early June
  • September to early December

Most expensive months:

  • Late December to early January (Holiday season)
  • June to August (Summer break)

Money-Saving Tips:

  • Fly on Tuesdays or Wednesdays for cheaper airfare
  • Stay on Maui or Big Island (cheaper than Oahu)
  • Use Airbnb or budget hotels instead of resorts

Hawaii Travel Guide: Monthly Breakdown

  • January to March: Best for whale watching & surfing but rainy.
  • April to June: Perfect mix of good weather, fewer crowds, and lower prices.
  • July to August: Hot, dry, busiest season with lots of festivals.
  • September to October: Best balance of cost, weather, and crowds.
  • November to December: Rainy, cheaper before mid-December, expensive for holidays.

Final Thoughts: When Should You Visit?

  • Best overall time: April to June and September to mid-December (Great weather, fewer crowds, and lower prices)
  • Best for surfing and whale watching: Winter (December to February)
  • Best for families: Summer (June to August)

No matter when you visit, Hawaii is always beautiful. Choose the season that best fits your travel style, and enjoy paradise!
